## 🚀 Quick Setup (15 Minutes)

### For Store Owners

#### Step 1: Complete Location Info (5 min)

Add to your store profile:
```
Store Name: Your Store Name
Location: New York, NY
Address: 123 Main Street
City: New York
State: NY
ZIP: 10001
Phone: (212) 555-0123
Email: contact@yourstore.com
```

#### Step 2: Add GPS Coordinates (2 min)

1. Find your store on Google Maps
2. Right-click on location
3. Click coordinates to copy
4. Add to store profile

**Example**: 40.7128, -74.0060

#### Step 3: Create Google My Business (8 min)

1. Go to: https://www.google.com/business/
2. Click "Manage now"
3. Add your business
4. Verify ownership
5. Complete profile

**Done!** ✅

## 🎯 Local SEO Best Practices

### 1. Use Location in Store Name
**Good**: "TechDeals Electronics - NYC"
**Bad**: "Store123"

### 2. Write Location-Rich Description
**Good**:
```
Welcome to TechDeals in New York City! 
Located in Manhattan, we serve customers 
throughout NYC, Brooklyn, and Queens. 
Visit us at 123 Main Street or shop 
online for local pickup.
```

**Bad**: "We sell electronics."

### 3. Add Location to Product Titles
**Good**: "iPhone 13 Pro - NYC Local Pickup"
**Bad**: "Phone"

### 4. Mention Local Pickup
Add to product descriptions:
- "Available for local pickup in [City]"
- "Free delivery within [City]"
- "Located in [Neighborhood]"

## 📊 Google My Business Setup

### Why It's Critical

Google My Business makes your store appear in:
- ✅ Google Maps
- ✅ Local search results
- ✅ "Near me" searches
- ✅ Google Knowledge Panel

### Quick Setup

1. **Create Account**: https://www.google.com/business/
2. **Add Business**: Enter store name and address
3. **Verify**: Receive postcard or phone call
4. **Complete Profile**: Add photos, hours, description
5. **Get Reviews**: Ask customers for Google reviews

**Time**: 15 minutes
**Cost**: FREE!

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "My store doesn't show in Store Locator"

**Check**:
1. ✅ Is store status "approved"?
2. ✅ Did you add location?
3. ✅ Did you add GPS coordinates?

**Fix**: Complete store location information

**Reason**: No stores in that city yet

**Fix**: Add stores to the city, page auto-generates

**Check**:
1. ✅ Did you create Google My Business?
2. ✅ Did you add location keywords?
3. ✅ Did you get reviews?
4. ✅ Has it been at least 1 month?

**Fix**: Follow setup steps, wait 1-3 months
