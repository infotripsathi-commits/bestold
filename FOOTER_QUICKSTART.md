# Quick Start: Footer Management

## What You Get

A professional footer on every page with:
- ✅ About Us section
- ✅ Quick links (About, Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions)
- ✅ Contact information (Address, Phone, Email)
- ✅ Social media icons (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter)
- ✅ Copyright notice

## How to Customize

### Step 1: Access Settings
1. Log in as admin
2. Go to **Admin Panel** (top navigation)
3. Click **Settings** in the admin menu

### Step 2: Update Content

The settings page has 4 tabs:

#### **General Tab**
- Site Name: "BestOld"
- Site Tagline: "Buy & Sell Second-Hand Goods"
- Copyright Text: "© 2026 BestOld. All rights reserved."

#### **Footer Content Tab**
- About Us Description: Write 2-3 sentences about your platform
- About Page URL: `/about` (create this page first)
- Privacy Policy URL: `/privacy-policy` (create this page first)
- Terms & Conditions URL: `/terms-conditions` (create this page first)

#### **Social Links Tab**
Add full URLs to your social profiles:
- Facebook: `https://facebook.com/yourpage`
- Instagram: `https://instagram.com/yourprofile`
- YouTube: `https://youtube.com/@yourchannel`
- Twitter: `https://twitter.com/yourhandle`

**Tip**: Leave empty to hide that social icon

#### **Contact Info Tab**
- Business Address: Your full address (can be multiple lines)
- Phone Number: `+1 (555) 123-4567`
- Email Address: `contact@bestold.com`

### Step 3: Save Changes
Click the **"Save Changes"** button (top-right or bottom of page)

### Step 4: View Footer
Visit any page on your site to see the updated footer!

## Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Use full URLs for social links (include `https://`)
- Keep about text short (2-3 sentences)
- Test all links after saving
- Only add social links you actively use

❌ **Don't:**
- Link to pages that don't exist yet
- Use incomplete URLs
- Add too much text in the about section
- Forget to save after making changes
