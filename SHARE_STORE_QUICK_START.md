# Share Store Feature - Quick Start Guide

## 🎉 What's New?

Store owners and customers can now easily share stores with friends, family, and on social media!

## 📍 Where to Find It

### For Customers
**Store Detail Page** - When viewing any store, look for the **"Share Store"** button at the top of the page, next to the Follow and Chat buttons.

### For Store Owners
1. **Seller Dashboard** - In the "Quick Actions" section
2. **Store Management Page** - Top right corner of the page

## 🚀 How to Use

### Quick Share (Mobile)
1. Click **"Share Store"** button
2. Your phone's share menu will open
3. Choose any app (WhatsApp, Messages, Email, etc.)
4. Send!

### Desktop Share
1. Click **"Share Store"** button
2. A dialog opens with options:
   - **Copy Link** - Click the copy button to copy store URL
   - **WhatsApp** - Share directly to WhatsApp
   - **Facebook** - Post on Facebook
   - **Twitter** - Tweet about the store
   - **Telegram** - Share on Telegram
   - **Email** - Send via email

## 💡 Share Options

| Platform | What Happens |
|----------|--------------|
| 📋 **Copy Link** | Copies store URL to clipboard - paste anywhere! |
| 💬 **WhatsApp** | Opens WhatsApp with pre-filled message |
| 👥 **Facebook** | Opens Facebook share dialog |
| 🐦 **Twitter** | Opens Twitter with pre-filled tweet |
| ✈️ **Telegram** | Opens Telegram share interface |
| 📧 **Email** | Opens email with store details |

## ✨ What Gets Shared?

When you share a store, the message includes:
- Store name
- Store description (first 100 characters)
- Direct link to the store page

Example:
```
Amazing Electronics Store - Quality second-hand electronics at unbeatable prices. All items tested and...
https://bestold.com/stores/abc123
```

## 💬 Tips for Effective Sharing

### Store Owners
1. **Optimize Your Description** - First 100 characters appear in shares
2. **Use Quality Images** - Banner image may show in previews
3. **Share Regularly** - Keep your store visible
4. **Track Results** - See which platforms bring customers

### Customers
1. **Add Personal Touch** - Tell friends why you recommend the store
2. **Be Specific** - Mention what products they have
3. **Share Responsibly** - Only recommend stores you trust
4. **Help Sellers** - Your shares help small businesses grow

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the Share button"
- **Solution**: Refresh the page (Ctrl+Shift+R)
- For sellers: Make sure your store is created

### "Copy link doesn't work"
- **Solution**: Manually select and copy the URL from the text field
- Or try a different browser

### "Native share doesn't appear on mobile"
- **Solution**: The dialog will appear instead - all features still work!

## 📊 What Happens When You Share?

1. **Click Share Button** → Dialog opens (or native share on mobile)
2. **Choose Platform** → Opens that platform's share interface
3. **Customize Message** → Add your personal touch (optional)
4. **Send/Post** → Share is complete!
5. **Recipients Click Link** → They visit the store page
